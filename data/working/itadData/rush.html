Price OverviewPrice Detail

Price Overview chart shows development of price over time
  • How often is this game on sale? Are deals getting better?
  • How quickly did it reach current historical low?
  • Is historical low being repeated?
  • Is the regular price of the game dropping?
  • Do all stores drop price?
Price Detail chart shows a per-store view of price history
  • When and how good deals did a store have?
  • Do all stores have deals at the same time?
  • How much prices differ between stores?
  • Are there any differences between sale frequency?
\r\n

Cut OverviewSavings

Cut Overview chart shows development of price cuts over time
  • How often is this game on sale? What is the deals cut?
  • How often are deals repeated?
  • How quickly did price cuts reached current level?
Savings chart shows absolute value of price cut
If regular price drops, same price cut won't mean same savings
\r\n

Sales DistributionSales Duration

Sales distribution chart can tell you when there usually is a sale during a year
  • When are deals more frequent?
  • Are deals with higher cut more frequent during certain time of year?
Note that frequency is aggregate over all historical data and all selected stores
Sales duration chart may tell you how long a sale usually lasts
  • Do deals with smaller cut last longer? By how much?
  • What is a median duration of a sale?
  • How many times was a game on sale with a price cut?
Note that frequency is aggregate over all historical data and all selected stores
