Price OverviewPrice Detail

Price Overview chart shows development of price over time

  • How much prices differ between stores?
  • Are there any differences between sale frequency?

Cut OverviewSavings

Cut Overview chart shows development of price cuts over time
  • How often is this game on sale? What is the deals cut?
  • How often are deals repeated?
  • How quickly did price cuts reached current level?
Savings chart shows absolute value of price cut
If regular price drops, same price cut won't mean same savings

Sales DistributionSales Duration

Sales distribution chart can tell you when there usually is a sale during a year
  • When are deals more frequent?
  • Are deals with higher cut more frequent during certain time of year?
Note that frequency is aggregate over all historical data and all selected stores
Sales duration chart may tell you how long a sale usually lasts
  • Do deals with smaller cut last longer? By how much?
  • What is a median duration of a sale?
  • How many times was a game on sale with a price cut?
Note that frequency is aggregate over all historical data and all selected stores

Arguably the most well-known indie game of all time, Cave Story features a completely original storyline wrapped with personality, mystery and hours of fast-paced fun. Cave Story is an action-adventure game from the critically acclaimed independent designer, Daisuke Amaya--or Pixel to his fans. Overflowing with unmatched charm and character, Cave Story takes you into a rare world where a curious race of innocent rabbit-like creatures, called Mimigas, run free.\r\rYou wake up in a dark cave with no memory of who you are, where you came from or why you're in such a place. Uncovering Mimiga Village you discover that the once-carefree Mimigas are in danger at the hands of a maniacal scientist. The Mimigas only hope rest squarely on the shoulders of a quiet, amnesiac boy who can't remember his own name.\r\rRun, jump, shoot, fly and explore your way through a massive action-adventure reminiscent of classic 8- and 16-bit games. Take control and learn the origins of this worlds power, stop the delusional villain and save the Mimiga!
